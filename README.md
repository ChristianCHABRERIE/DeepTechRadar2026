# DeepTech Radar 2026

Access the interactive mind map here: https://christianchabrerie.github.io/DeepTechRadar2026/

## Overview

The DeepTech Radar 2026 is a comprehensive knowledge repository visualizing the current state and future trajectory of deep technologies. Centered around the Consumer Electronics Show (CES) 2026, this project aggregates critical data points, trends, and innovations into a structured mind map format, allowing for intuitive exploration of complex technological ecosystems.

## Content and Scope

This repository provides a detailed industry analysis based on extensive data collection. Users exploring the radar will find structured information covering:

*   **Global Tech Trends**: Analysis of strategic technology trends for 2025 and beyond, including insights from Gartner's Hype Cycle and other major industry reports.
*   **Artificial Intelligence Landscape**: A deep dive into the AI ecosystem, featuring key players such as Mistral, OpenAI, Google DeepMind, and emerging sovereign AI initiatives in Europe and Asia.
*   **CES 2026 Focus**: Comprehensive coverage of the event, including Innovation Awards, keynotes, and significant announcements across sectors like Robotics, Mobility, and Smart Home.
*   **Sector-Specific Analysis**:
    *   **HealthTech**: Innovations in longevity, wearable sensors, and preventive healthcare.
    *   **Computing**: Developments in neuromorphic computing, energy-efficient AI, and semiconductor advancements.
    *   **Mobility**: Evolution of autonomous vehicles, EVs, and related infrastructure.
*   **Ecosystem Mapping**: Tracking of startups, academic institutions (such as EPITA), and national delegations (La French Tech) contributing to the deep tech landscape.

## Participative Project

This repository serves as a collaborative platform for technology monitoring. We encourage contributions from the community to ensure the radar remains accurate and up-to-date.

Subject matter experts and enthusiasts are invited to propose additions or corrections:
1.  Fork the repository.
2.  Update the markdown source files with verifiable references.
3.  Submit a Pull Request for review.

Our goal is to maintain a high-quality, verified database of deep tech innovations to serve researchers, investors, and technology professionals.
